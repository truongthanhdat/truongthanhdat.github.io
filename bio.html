Brief Bio

Dat Truong received his B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the Honors Program at the University of Science, VNU-HCM in 2019 under supervisions of Professor Khoa Luu and Professor Minh-Triet Tran. He is currently the Ph.D. student the University of Arkansas under the supervision of Prof. Khoa Luu.

In Summer 2018, he did an internship at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign under the supervision of Professor Minh Do where he mainly focused on Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), particularly, his majority time concentrated on Unsupervised Image to Image Translation via GANs which have been strongly raised in the research community for recent years.

Dat Truong's research interest widely includes Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Generative Model, and Theoretical Deep Learning. He also used to be an ACM ICPC contestant in 2015.
