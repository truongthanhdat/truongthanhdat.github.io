Brief Bio

I am a senior student of University of Science, VNU-HCMC. I graduated from Nguyen Dinh Chieu High School for the Gifted. I am learning Information Technology. My current major is Computer Science. My research fields include Pattern Recognition, Multimedia Retrieval, Lifelogging Retrieval. Additionally, I am interested in Algorithm and Data Structure. Next year, I am going to finish my university under supervising of A.Prof Minh-Triet Tran and M.Sc Vinh-Tiep Nguyen.
