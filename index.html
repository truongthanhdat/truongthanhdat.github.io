Dat Truong is currently a Ph.D. student at the University of Arkansas under the supervision of Professor Khoa Luu. He received his B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Honors Program, Faculty of Information Technology, University of Science, VNU in 2019 under supervisions of Professor Khoa Luu , and Professor Minh-Triet Tran. He was an intern with Professor Minh Do at Coordinated Science Laboratory at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2018.

His research interest widely includes Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision. He also used to be an ACM ICPC contestant with a solid background in Algorithms and Data Structures.
